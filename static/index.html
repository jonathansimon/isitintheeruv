About

This site was created by Jonathan Simon while he and his wife were looking for a home in Berkeley, California. (You guessed it, that's why the site only supports the Berkeley eruv at the moment). Hopefully others will find it useful as well. In the future, we hope to include other eruvim from other communities as well as walking distances to additional points of interest.
